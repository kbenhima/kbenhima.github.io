Completed papers

Foreign Exchange Interventions with UIP and CIP deviations: The Case of Small Safe Haven Economies
2025, with Philippe Bacchetta and Brendan Berthold, R&R Review of Economic Studies
Foreign exchange interventions entail a net risk-adjusted benefit to small safe haven economies because foreign intermediaries value the safe haven property of the domestic currency more than domestic households.
Short-term Finance, Long-term Effects
2024, with Omar Chafik, Min Fang and Wenxia Tang, R&R JPE: Macro
We calibrate a heterogeneous-firm model of short-term loan guarantees using Moroccan data. The gains from expanding the program by lowering entry costs or increasing the guaranteed ratio are substantial.
The International Transmission of Asset Market Shocks in Liquidity Traps
2024, with Philippe Bacchetta, Yannick Kalantzis and Maxime Phillot, R&R Journal of International Economics
A decrease in the supply of assets issued abroad or an increase in the demand for assets leads to an asset shortage domestically. In normal times, the nominal interest rate decreases, stimulating investment and output. In a liquidity trap, deflation hits instead and the currency appreciates, causing a recession.
